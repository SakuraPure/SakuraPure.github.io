背背背…

应用文

Dear 人名,

​ My name is ____and I am____.信息点1.I’m writing this letter on the behalf of___to___.(站在什么的立场)(常用:Aologize Recommaend Apply for congratulate suggest inquire)The followings are the details.

Sincerely yours,

______人名______

通知

Notice

September 11th

​ This notice is for the purpose of____通知目的____cdoing sth________.The forthcoming event(替换为事件) aimed at ____doing sth____.The followings are the details.(以下为具体内容)

​ 第二段三到四个句子——一般为时间、地点、活动内容、注意事项

例:Every participant invovled must attend the event on time at 7:00 pm.(时间)The duration of this activity is chosen as the site.Please bring your IDs to be checked in the front door.(地点)Last but not least,there will be some interesting agendas,which you must pay attention to.For example,votes and Q&A.(活动内容)

​ 第三段:呼吁/答疑+联系方式

​ If you have any questions,you are free to contact at 192389 in the working hours.

​ 落款

​ Student Union

新闻报道

  • 段落1——结果,事件本身

    要素：时间，地点，参与者，事件本身，事件结果。

    客观用第三人称(thy,he,she,student union),过去式(一般过去时表示事件的结果，过去进行时表示事件的背景 was were doing)

    On December First,Monday,the Student Union organized a meaningful activity to visit the local farm,in which 300 volunteers actively participated.It turns out to be a great success.

  • 段落2——三个细节

    活动目的+三个具体时间+三句补足+以时间顺序串联

    This activity was aimed at doing sth(目的) by doing sth

    When asked about those volunteers’ feelings about such an experience,all of them responded with a smile,saying“what a wonderful practice and I really appreciate this experience,for it makes me learn to use my knowledge in practice.”

  • 段落3——新闻意义

    This activity turns out to be a beneficial event for both sides.The Student Union expressed that there will be more meaningful events in the future.结尾选择2：More efforts will be made to help farmers in rural areas.
