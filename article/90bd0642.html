  • 论点1 first and foremost,there is no doubt that______.
  • 论据1Based on big data,a majority of successful social elites admitted that they spent more than 2/3 of their time in doing sth.
  • 论点2 furthermore(moreover,additionally),it is obvious that_____.
  • 论据2 where there is/are_____,there is are_____.
  • 论点3 last but not least,no one can deny that_____.
  • l论据3 Although_____,_____.虽然但是。

第三段:

In a word,the whole society should pay close attention to the problem of_____.Only in this way can we have a bright and promising future.

一、现象解释

第一段:

With the rapid development of ____,it is of great necessity for_____to_____.It is quite natural for us to ponder over the question of ____/that+从句.

二、观点选择

第一段:

Nowadays (youngsters/students)have different options upon graduation:_____________.(阐述问题、背景)Some think that_____is beneficial,while others think_____ is a better choice.I prefer the former/latter.The following reasons can account for my preference.

三、问题解决

第一段:

第一段:

As an old saying goes:_____.For us college students,it has an increasingly important significance:_____解释含义.The following reasons can account for this issue.
