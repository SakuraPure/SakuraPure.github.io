英语笔记1

2021年10月12日 374 字 大概 1 分钟

不记就忘

  1. obligation n.义务责任

  2. internal adj.内部的

  3. inherently adv.固有的

  4. underlying adj.潜在的

  5. indicate v.表明，标示，指出，暗示

  6. cue v.暗示

  7. reinforcement positive/negative reinforcement:

    positive reinforcement is when you give someone praise or rewards for their behavior or work, so they want to continue doing well(鼓励)。Negative reinforcement is when you give someone punishments or criticism when their behavior or work is bad, so that they want to improve to avoid punishments again。

  8. take one’s mind off 转移注意力，使某人不再去想（担忧）某事

  9. go hand in hand 密切相关

  10. self-reproach 自责

  11. be nice to 对某人好

  12. suck the life out of you 令你的生活糟糕

  13. manifest v.表明，显示

  14. pursuit n.追求

    in pursuit of = pursue

  15. assess = evaluate v.评估，评价

  16. address v.表达

  17. cling to 坚持

  18. let go of 放手，放弃

  19. decisive adj.果断的，有决断力的

  20. reinforcer n.强化物

  21. authorize v.授予，授权

  22. circuitous adj.迂回的，曲折的，间接的

  23. role modeled 榜样

  24. baggage in hand 带来负担

  25. solidify v.固化

  26. deal with 处理，应付，交往

  27. Better yet,just do it. 然而

  28. be resolute in 坚决

  29. 基础 underlie 事物

  30. largely adj.很大程度上

  31. meet/fulfill one’s obligation 完成任务

  32. Play a role of 起了…作用

  33. exploit v.利用

  34. resolute = firm = determined

  35. lines n.台词

  36. intense adj.紧张的，激烈的

  37. fairly adj.相当的

  38. put arm around 搂着

  39. share n.股份

  40. pleasing adj.惹人喜爱的

  41. invest v.投资

  42. lottery tickets 彩票

  43. reality n.现实

  44. diploma n.毕业文凭

  45. circumstance n.环境，状况，条件

  46. capital n.财产

  47. reason v.推理思考

  48. transform v.改变

  49. perspective n.态度观点

  50. embrace v.采纳

  51. open up to 接触
